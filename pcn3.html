Description

Presented poetry-collection "Kavya Kunj Kalika" compiled by My Xitiz Poetry. This is an inspirational collection of poems aimed at promoting mutual exchange between Hindi and non-Hindi speaking areas. It includes poems by young poets from different regions of India, written in both Hindi and English, to encourage and reward them.

The poems explore various themes such as hope, joy, aspirations, satire, and sorrow. The collection aims to touch hearts and bring out deep human emotions. The poems reflect the experiences of young poets, many of whom have moved from rural areas to cities, yet have maintained their authenticity and creativity. A must-read for poetry lovers looking for emotional and thought-provoking content.
