Description

In the midst of questions about the true meaning of love, Samaira finds herself at rock bottom after losing everything dear to her. With a shattered heart and uncertain desires, she navigates through life without direction. On the other hand, Vivian, a driven entrepreneur aspiring for greatness, crosses paths with Samaira, igniting an undeniable connection. Intrigued by her enigmatic aura, he becomes determined to restore her passion and purpose. Together, they embark on a captivating journey of rediscovery, chasing broken dreams against all odds.
